Creative Work

UGC Ad Framework

🪝 Hook Strategy
  • Open with a problem or curiosity gap
  • "Have an app idea but no developer?"
  • "Want smarter investment decisions with less work?"
  • Goal: Stop the scroll in first 2 seconds
  • Pattern-interrupt visual or bold statement opener
🧠 Persuasion Elements
  • Social proof: "Join successful students and founders"
  • Urgency: "Limited-time offer" or "Free trial available"
  • Benefit-driven: Focus on outcomes, not features
  • Clear CTA: "Sign up," "Book demo," "Try free"
  • Risk reversal: Money-back guarantee framing
🧪 A/B Testing Framework
  • Test hook variations (problem vs. curiosity vs. benefit)
  • Test copy length (short vs. detailed)
  • Test CTA buttons (Sign up vs. Learn more vs. Book demo)
  • Measure performance and scale winners
  • Document learning for future iterations
